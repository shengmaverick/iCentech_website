iCentech has been selected as the language service provider for collecting and translating recall information for the automotive and consumer products industries across multiple countries. This allows the client to stay up-to-date with global recall situations and effectively track developments in the domestic Chinese market. Our tailored solution ensures accurate and timely translations, helping the client maintain compliance and provide transparent information to consumers in China.

Solution:

iCentech delivered a customized and flexible workflow to meet the client’s specific needs, ensuring both speed and accuracy. Our solution included:

  • Tailored Project Management: A dedicated Project Manager was assigned to oversee all processes, ensuring timely and high-quality deliveries.
  • Expert Translation: We utilized experienced translators to ensure linguistic accuracy and consistent terminology across all languages.
  • Custom Tool Development: Our team developed specialized tools to collect recall information and handle various file formats, improving efficiency and minimizing errors.
  • Machine Translation Integration: By leveraging advanced machine translation (MT) technology for initial translations, combined with human post-editing, we enhanced both speed and quality, meeting tight deadlines.

Languages:

  • Japanese to Simplified Chinese
  • Korean to Simplified Chinese
